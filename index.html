Daehwa Kim

I am a research associate at Future Interfaces Group, Human-Computer Interaction Institute, Carnegie Mellon University. Formerly, I did my masters at KAIST HCIL, School of Computing, where I had a great opportunity to work with professor Geehyuk Lee. My research goal is engineering for a fluid interface, exploring a seamless integration of a computer and human that makes new and pleasant experiences. My research interests lie in sensing technologies, sensor fabrication, and AR/VR/MR interfaces. I presented full papers at ACMCHI and UIST and have received an Honorable Mention award at CHI 2021.

I am actively looking for Ph.D. positions for Fall 2022!
